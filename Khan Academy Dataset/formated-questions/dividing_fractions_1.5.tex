\section*{Dividing positive fractions}
\textbf{Question.} $\frac{4}{6}$ \textbackslash\{\}div $\frac{9}{2}$ = \{?\}

\textbf{Answer.} 0.14814814814814814

\textbf{Hints.}
\begin{itemize}

  \item The reciprocal of $\frac{9}{2}$ is $\frac{2}{9}$.
