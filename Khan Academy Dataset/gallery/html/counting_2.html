  • Given 5 people, each person shakes the hands of 4 other people.

  • The following is almost the answer. 5 \times 4 = 20

  • We have double counted the handshakes though, since Alex shaking Alex’s hand is the same handshake as Alex shaking Alex’s hand.

  • Therefore, the following is the correct answer. \frac{5 $\textbackslash\{\}times$ 4}{2} = [[NUM_COLORS * NUM_POTS]]
